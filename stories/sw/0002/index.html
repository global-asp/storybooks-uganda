Tazama wanyama Look at the animals Tazama wanyama

Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

Caren Echesa

Kiswahili

Level 1

Tazama wanyama.

Look at the animals.

Tazama wanyama.

Ng'ombe anasema, "Moo."

The cow says, "Moo."

Ng'ombe anasema, "Moo."

Mbuzi anasema, "Mee, mee."

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

Mbuzi anasema, "Mee, mee."

Farasi anasema, "Nei."

The horse says, "Neigh."

Farasi anasema, "Nei."

Nguruwe anasema, "Ghuranti."

The pig says, "Grunt."

Nguruwe anasema, "Ghuranti."

Kuku anasema, "Ko, ko, ko."

The chicken says, "Cluck."

Kuku anasema, "Ko, ko, ko."

Mbwa anasema, "Bwe, bwe."

The dog says, "Woof."

Mbwa anasema, "Bwe, bwe."

Mkulima anasema,"Nyamaza!"

The farmer says, "Shhh!"

Mkulima anasema,"Nyamaza!"

Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Caren Echesa
Language: Kiswahili
Level 1
