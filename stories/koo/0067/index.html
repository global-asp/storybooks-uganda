Erihuka Cooking Kupika

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Kathy Arbuckle

Amos Mubunga Kambere

Lukhonzo

Level 1

Ngahata ebiribwa

I peel the potatoes.

Ninamenya viazi.

Ngasarasara esyanyinyi

I chop a cabbage.

Ninakatakata kabichi.

Ngasaranga esya caroti

I grate the carrots.

Ninasaga karoti.

Ngowaya ebihimba

I wash the beans.

Ninaosha maharagwe.

Ngasara ebinyobwa

I cut the butternut.

Ninakata mbegu za maboga.

Ngasaranga esyanyinyi

I chop the spinach.

Ninakatakata sukuma-wiki.

Mama wayi akasaranga ebitunguru

My mom chops the onions.

Mama yangu anakatakata vitunguu.

Ebitunguru bikaletha emisoni ukabisaranga

Onions make me cry when they are chopped.

Vitunguu hunifanya nilie vikiwa vinakatwakatwa.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Kathy Arbuckle
Translated by: Amos Mubunga Kambere
Language: Lukhonzo
Level 1
Source: Cooking from African Storybook
