Omuliro Fire Moto

Deborah Namugosa, Beatres Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano

Rob Owen

Lunyole

Level 1

Wona omuliro!

Look, fire!

Angalia, moto!

Gwohya.

Fire burns.

Moto unachoma.

Gunyiya.

Fire cooks.

Moto unatumika kupika.

Gubasa nihuŋulirisa engano.

Fire gives warmth.

Moto unaleta joto.

Gutuŋa obutaanghafu.

Fire gives light.

Moto unaleta mwanga.

Wona omuliro!

Look, fire!

Angalia, moto!

Omuliro sigulomehaho.

Fire is wonderful.

Moto ni wa ajabu.

Era gwamaani.

And it is powerful.

Na una nguvu sana.

Written by: Deborah Namugosa, Beatres Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano
Illustrated by: Rob Owen
Language: Lunyole
Level 1
