Umusatsi Hair Nywele

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter

Patrick Munyurangabo

Kinyarwanda

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Thuli afite umusatsi mutoya /mugufi.

Thuli has short hair.

Thuli ana nywele fupi.

Anna afite umusatsi muremure.

Anna has long hair.

Anna ana nywele ndefu.

Cathy afite umusatsi muremure kurusha bose.

Cathy has the longest hair.

Cathy ana nywele ndefu kuliko wote.

Zama yaryamishije umusatsi we.

Zama has plaited her hair.

Zama amesuka nywele zake.

Baba afite ubwanwa.

Baba has a beard.

Baba ana ndevu.

Zanele yasokoje umusatsi we.

Zanele has combed her hair.

Zanele amechana nywele zake.

Thabo yacase umusatsi we.

Thabo has cut his hair.

Thabo amenyoa nywele zake.

Themba yogoshe umusatsi we.

Themba has shaved his hair.

Themba amenyoa kipara.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Patrick Munyurangabo
Language: Kinyarwanda
Level 1
