Imyenda y’ishuli. School clothes Sare za shule

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini

Patrick Munyurangabo

Kinyarwanda

Level 1

Iyi kanzu ni ndende.

This dress is long.

Gauni hili ni refu.

Uyu mupira ni munini.

This jersey is big.

Sweta hii ni kubwa.

Iki gikapu ni kinini.

This bag is big.

Mkoba huu ni mkubwa.

Uyu mukandara ni…

This belt is...

Mkanda huu ni …

Iyi ngofero ni ntoya.

This hat is small.

Kofia hii ni ndogo.

Amasogisi ni magufi.

The socks are short.

Soksi hizi ni fupi.

Ariko izi nkweto ni shya.

But these shoes are new.

Lakini viatu hivi ni vipya.

… zirakwira neza.

... they fit well.

… vinanitosha vizuri.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini
Translated by: Patrick Munyurangabo
Language: Kinyarwanda
Level 1
